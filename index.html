Why ProcrastiMate?

Designed to assist IBDP students struggling with chronic procrastination, our platform offers a routine planner, and motivation.
Looking at the Routine Planner ProcrastiMate's Routine Planner is designed to help you structure your day efficiently by organizing tasks, setting priorities, and tracking progress.
With an intuitive drag-and-drop interface, smart scheduling, and customizable time blocks, you can create a routine that fits your lifestyle.
Whether you're managing study sessions, work schedules, or personal goals, ProcrastiMate ensures you stay on track and make the most of your time.
